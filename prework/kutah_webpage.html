Who is Kutah?

Kutah is a Chicago native that has always worked in education. She was a Teach for America corp member and taught 3rd-5th grade Diverse Learning (formerly known as Special Education) at an elementary school. Diverse Learning classrooms reqire differentiation and this is frequently achieved with online educational programs. While these programs were helpful, Kutah saw ways they could be improved. When Kutah decided to stop being a teacher, she considered applying for roles at Edtech companies, but ultimately took the Case Manager position at a different elementary school. For the past 2 years, Kutah has been leading the Diverse Learning department at her school. Working in education during a pandemic has illuminated 1) that Kutah does not enjoy working for a school and 2) the importance of online educational programs. Kutah has joined Coding Temple with the goal of abtaining a job in the EdTech industry.
